Sleep

Deep sleep averages ~80 min (normal). The real problem is late, irregular bedtimes → short sleep → lost REM. Deep sleep is front-loaded; REM is in the early-morning hours you cut off by sleeping late.

The fix — anchors first

  • 1 · Morning bright light within 30–60 min of waking — strongest lever to shift your clock earlier
  • 2 · Fixed wake time daily — don't sleep in to "catch up"
  • Move bedtime earlier gradually (15–30 min every few nights)
  • Wind-down + screens off 1–2 h before bed (Mindful Cooldown)
  • Caffeine cutoff early afternoon · no evening nicotine · cool dark room
  • Sleep on your side, head slightly raised (OSA + reflux)

Trazodone stopped → expect lighter sleep for a week or two; behavioural tools now do the work. Magnesium → move to AM if it wakes you.
